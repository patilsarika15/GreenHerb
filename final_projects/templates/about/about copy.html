GreenHerb

Plant species identification is a challenging task that has a key role in effectively studying biodiversity and investigating unknown species. The manual identification of plant species is a time-consuming process and requires a lot of expertise in the field. Automated identification systems based on computer vision and machine learning techniques provide an alternative and assistive approach for this task. These systems are useful, but their accuracy varies due to the diversity of the species
